What is ReeldIn?

Movies are a highly valued creative outlet and pastime, but frequently, it's hard to choose one. With millions available and more releasing daily, the hurdle of picking something to watch can be daunting. ReeldIn intends to match users with movies they'll enjoy. Our product is sleek, quick. and efficient. We use a combination of user input and an extensive movie database to provide the best recommendations possible. We're here to make your movie-watching experience as enjoyable as possible.


FAQ


Q: How do I make an account?


A: To make an account, click the "Login" button in the top right corner of the page. You'll be prompted to input your email and password. Once you've done that, you'll have access to all of ReeldIn's features.


Q: How do I start getting recommendations?


A: To get recommendations click on the "Get Recommendations" button the top right of the page.


Q: How does ReeldIn work?


A: ReeldIn uses user input and an extensive movie database to recommendations. Users can input their favorite movies and genres and ReeldIn will use that information to suggest similar movies.
